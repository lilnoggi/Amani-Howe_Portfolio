SplitLight

SplitLight is a 2D atmospheric puzzle-platformer made during the 2025 Pixel Game Jam under the theme "Light is Dangerous." You awaken in a forgotten dreamspace with no memory — only a fragile lantern to guide your way. Every step forward uncovers haunting memories and twisted reflections of the self. But beware: the more you reveal, the more the darkness sees you.

I worked alongside talented artists, a composer, and another programmer to create a uniquely eerie experience. Check out the project on Itch.io or dive into the GitHub repo.

My Role

  • Bug fixing and jam-time polish
