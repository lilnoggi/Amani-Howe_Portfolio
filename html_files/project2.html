Game Design Document (GDD) for Brainrot Tycoon (working title)

ðŸŽ¯Game concept

A chaotic, internet-themed idle/tycoon game where you build a growing "brainrot empire" by clicking for Brainrot, buying silly upgrades, and expanding a colourful isometric world full of cursed internet landmarks.

  • Click to generate Brainrot Manually.
  • Buy upgrades to passively generate Brainrot over time.
  • Expand your base by spawning meme factories, TikTok dance floors, Discord servers, internt shrines, and more.
  • Unlock Shayla characters to provide boosts or special bonuses.
  • Earn Shayla Creds (premium currency) to buy super cursed upgrades.

Tone: Silly, chaotic, meme-core, pastel/neon colours, constant brainrot energy.

ðŸŽ®Core Gameplay Loop

  1. Click the Big Brainrot Button to generate Brainrot manually.
  2. Spend Brainrot in the Shop to buy upgrades (buildings/machines).
  3. Buildings are automatically placed into the world.
  4. Buildings Generate Brainrot passively over time.
  5. Expand your empire, unlocking more upgrades, boosting your Brainrot production.
  6. Use Shayla Creds to unlock special mega-upgrades or powerful characters.

ðŸ’°Currencies

  • Brainrot: Main currency, earned by clicking or passively.
  • Shayla Creds: Special currency earned through milestones, used for high-tier upgrades.
